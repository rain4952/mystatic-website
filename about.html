About Us

Nissan is a leader in the automotive industry, known for innovation, performance, and engineering excellence. Our mission is to provide vehicles that deliver thrilling driving experiences while prioritizing safety, sustainability, and advanced technology. We have a rich heritage, and our commitment to quality has earned us a dedicated global following. At Nissan, we continually push the boundaries to create vehicles that embody performance, style, and value.

The Nissan Skyline R34, launched in the late 1990s, is an iconic symbol of performance and engineering excellence. Renowned for its advanced technology, including the ATTESA all-wheel-drive system and the powerful RB26DETT engine, the R34 delivers thrilling performance and precise handling. Its striking design and cultural impact, particularly in films and video games, have made it a beloved choice among enthusiasts.
